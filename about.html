About The Auracratic Party

President - Leo Zelinski

As a high school student deeply passionate about bridging communication gaps, I've embarked on a journey to create a video chat transcription and translation service. My motivation stems from a desire to dismantle language barriers that often hinder seamless interaction among people from diverse linguistic backgrounds. This project is not just a technical challenge for me; it's a personal mission to make the world a more connected place. Outside of this endeavor, I channel my energy and teamwork skills into playing soccer, where I find another form of universal language that brings people together, regardless of where they come from or what language they speak.
